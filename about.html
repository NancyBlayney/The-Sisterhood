So What's the Story?

We are four women who love tech. We are currently enrolled in the New York Academy of Coding + Design.

We are collaborating on this web page, each in our own way, to bring together our different styles. We care deeply that all girls and women have a chance to learn technology so that it may enrich their lives. Knowledge is power!

We Are:

Bellatix

After killing Sirius Black with a well-aimed Stunning Spell, Bellatrix Lestrange left magic behind for good. She had her wand snapped in half by the proper authorities and started on a long journey of self-discovery. She discovered a love for technology after attending a "GIRLS CODE IT" workshop hosted by a group of young witches in Diagon Alley. Since then, Bellatrix has worked in the IT departments of organizations such as Comcast, BP, and the Bush White House. She hopes to use her technology powers only for evil.

Najee

Najee aka NINJA CODE WARRIOR was found outside after slipping into a mysterious green toxic chemical called " CHEMICAL X". She woke up with a new found super power. The power to CODE and the power to make people do her bidding ! With her new Found powers, life would never be the same! Najee became Ninja CODE Warrior. She vowed to use her powers of Code to change the technology world and her powers of persuasion to force people to buy her snicker bars!! Ninja now goes by the name Najee Bartey and works for Google as a programmer. Coding by day, eating snickers by night.

Lois

Lois Lane is a Pulitzer Prize-winning journalist for the Daily Planet of Metropolis. She is the primary love interest of Superman and was one of the first reporter to interview Superman, and remains one of his closest allies. She recently quit journalism to pursue a career as a web developer. Lois enjoys Zumba, biking, and traveling around the world with her hunky-superhero-boyfriend.

Raquel

A Czechoslovakian-born American politician and diplomat. She is the first woman to have become the United States Secretary of State. For her next career she decided to focus on technology so she can conquor the world.
