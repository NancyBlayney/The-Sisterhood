Donate Month to Tech Girlz

With your monthly donation, we can enable Tech Girlz to keep their workshops free. This is vital to ensuring that the next generation has access to the resources they need to diversify the tech community and bring new perspective to the challenges we face in the field. We want these young women to have the opportunities we never had, and your monthly contribution will solidify their place at the table at tech companies all across the world.
